lasagna

Description

To make lasagna, start by preparing a meat sauce with ground beef, tomato sauce, onions, and garlic, letting it simmer for full flavor. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg and seasonings. In a baking dish, layer lasagna noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella cheese, repeating until the dish is filled. Finish with a layer of sauce and cheese on top, then bake at 375°F for about 45 minutes, or until bubbly and golden.

ingredient

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Seasonings (salt, pepper, Italian herbs)

steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce by browning ground beef with onions and garlic, then add tomato sauce and seasonings. Let it simmer.
  2. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg and add seasonings.
  3. In a baking dish, layer lasagna noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  4. Repeat the layers until the dish is filled, finishing with sauce and cheese on top.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 45 minutes, or until bubbly and golden on top.
