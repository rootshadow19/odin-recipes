pizza

Description

To make pizza, start by preparing a dough with flour, yeast, water, and salt, letting it rise until doubled. Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough, then add toppings like mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, and vegetables. Bake in a preheated oven at 475°F for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is melted and bubbly.

ingredient

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Desired toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)
  • Olive oil (optional for crust)

steps

  1. Make the dough by mixing flour, yeast, water, and salt, then let it rise until it doubles in size.
  2. Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and your desired toppings.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 475°F (245°C) for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
